About our product

Our product is a lunch that comes in different presentations, it is made with top quality and very healthy ingredients, in ready-to-eat or ready-to-take home points of sale, it allows its refrigeration and subsequent consumption due to its thermo-sealed packaging.

We can take as a strength the fact that people have busy lives and we can sell our ready-to-eat product to them. In addition, we can work out a way to offering a free home distribution service which help us to boost sales and in turn make the consumer's life easier, which will undoubtedly corner our competitors.

The large number of tourist centers and attractions are a plus since it gives us the opportunity to establish a great number of points of sale to promote and position our products and services.
